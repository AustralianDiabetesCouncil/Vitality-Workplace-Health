What you'll get from the test

Quick and easy to complete

It is quick (15-20 minutes) and easy to complete. You receive an instant profile of your health and wellbeing so you know what areas require some simple changes, ultimately leading to better Health and increased Vitality.

Get an individualised profile

Your individualised profile also includes information and tips from Australian Diabetes Council's expert health professionals, to make it easier for you to start making small and simple lifestyle changes straight away.

An accurate assesment of your risks

You will receive accurate assessment of your risk of developing Type 2 Diabetes - giving you a unique opportunity to Act Now to prevent or delay this disease and in doing so, reduce your risk of other chronic diseases.

Why take the survey

  • Those who have or are at risk of diabetes (and other chronic diseases) often are not aware of any symptoms or early warning signs. The Vitality Health and Wellbeing Survey offers you the ideal opportunity to "get in early and take meaningful action" to reduce your risk and if you already have been diagnosed, to better manage your condition.
  • Acting sooner rather than later, with small easy lifestyle changes can transform the way your health plays out throughout your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is this survey about?

This survey is about giving you a clearer picture of your health. Because we know good health can't be measured on the scales alone.

Apart from the fact you are highly intelligent and extremely attractive. You would take this survey to give you a boarder view of your health and the behaviours that help shape it.

What is Vitality Workplace Health?

Vitality Workplace Health is a Health and Wellbeing program developed by the Australian Diabetes Council. The program aims to boost the health of employees and combat the ever increasing rate of diabetes and preventable chronic disease within the working population. Boosting the health of employees can significantly boost productivity, cut absenteeism and save companies thousands!
